My Swimming History

2016

First Swimming Lesson

Started my swimming journey with my first swimming lesson. This was the beginning of a passion that would shape my life and athletic career.

2019

COVID-19 & Beijing Training

Due to COVID-19, I was in Beijing where I trained under a national team coach. This experience elevated my training and I made swimming my main sport to focus on.

2021

Joined PSC Competitive Club

Started training with the local competitive club PSC. This marked my entry into serious competitive swimming.

2022

Athletic Honors Qualification

Qualified for the Athletic Honors, recognizing my dedication and improvement in competitive swimming.

2023 (Early)

First ISF Pool Record - U13 200IM LC

Broke my first ISF pool record in the U13 200 Individual Medley Long Course with a time of 2:59. A significant milestone in my swimming career!

2023 (Nov)

Hong Kong Age Group LC Championships

Qualified for and competed in the Hong Kong age group LC championships for the first time, placing 22nd.

2024 (Feb)

Hong Kong Age Group SC Championships

Competed in the Hong Kong age group SC (Short Course) championships again, improving to 17th place.

2024 (Jun)

Intensive Training Camp - Hangzhou

Attended an intensive swimming training camp in Hangzhou to further develop my skills and technique.

2024 (Aug)

Switched to Intensive Training Team

Transitioned to a more intensive training team within PSC to push myself further and compete at higher levels.

2024 (Oct)

HKSSF Champion

Won HKSSF championships in 2 individual events and 1 relay event. A major achievement in my competitive career!

2024 (Dec)

ACAMIS MVP - First Overseas Competition

Named ACAMIS MVP for the ISF swim team during my first overseas competition. An incredible honor!

2025 (Jan)

Division 1 Championships - Hong Kong Team Qualification

Competed in Division 1 championships, placing 6th and 12th in different events. Qualified for the Hong Kong team!

2025 (Feb)

Hong Kong Age Group SC Championships

Competed in the Hong Kong age group SC championships, achieving 9th place. Continuous improvement!

2025 (Jun)

Intensive Training Camp - Hangzhou (Injury Period)

Attended another intensive training camp in Hangzhou. Unfortunately, sustained injuries: lateral meniscus tear, shoulder overuse injury, and elbow tendonitis. A challenging period of recovery and rehabilitation.

2025 (Sep)

Qualified for National Level 1 Swimmer

After recovery from my injuries, qualified for national level 1 swimmer status. A testament to my resilience and dedication!

2025 (Oct)

HKSSF Division 1 Finals Qualification

Qualified for HKSSF Division 1 finals with placements of 5th, 6th, and 8th in different events.

2025 (Nov)

Hong Kong Age Group LC Championships - 4th Place

Competed in the Hong Kong age group LC championships and achieved 4th place. Getting closer to the podium!

2025 (Dec)

ACAMIS Senior Record Broken - Boys 4x50 Medley Relay

Broke the ACAMIS senior record in the Boys 4x50 Medley Relay. A significant achievement for the team and myself!

2026 (Jan)

Return from Injury - New Beginnings

I have returned from my injury period, and our trip down memory lane ends here. Looking forward to many more swim meets and hopefully achieving my first podium stand in the championships!
